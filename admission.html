Admission Procedure

Follow these easy steps to secure your child's admission to Kushinagar Public School.

1

Fill Application

Complete the online application form below or collect the prospectus form from the school office.

2

Submit required documents: birth certificate, previous class marksheet, transfer certificate (if applicable), and passport photos.

3

Entrance Assessment

Students for Class I and above may appear for a brief interaction/assessment to ensure appropriate grade placement.
