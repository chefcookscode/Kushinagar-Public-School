Who We Are

Our Introduction

Kushinagar Public School (KPS) is a premier CBSE-affiliated institution located at Amwa Kubersthan, Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh. Established with the vision of providing holistic, quality education, KPS has grown to become one of the most respected schools in the region.

We offer classes from Nursery to Class XII (10+2) under the CBSE curriculum. Our school is built on the philosophy that education is not merely the acquisition of knowledge, but its reflection in character, values, and daily conduct.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and a nurturing environment, Kushinagar Public School strives to bring out the best in every student — academically, physically, and emotionally.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence that nurtures globally competitive, socially responsible, and morally upright citizens — individuals who contribute meaningfully to society and lead with compassion, integrity, and purpose.

Our Mission

To provide a disciplined, caring, and inspiring educational environment where every student discovers their potential. We are committed to academic excellence, character development, and equipping students with 21st-century skills for lifelong success.
