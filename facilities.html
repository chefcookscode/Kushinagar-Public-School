School Infrastructure

Computer Laboratory

Our state-of-the-art computer laboratory is equipped with 40 modern desktop computers with high-speed internet connectivity, enabling students to develop essential digital skills from an early age.

The lab runs structured courses in programming, word processing, spreadsheets, and internet safety — preparing students for a digital-first world.

40 high-performance computers
High-speed broadband internet
Latest educational software
Dedicated experienced instructor

Science Laboratories

Our dedicated Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories provide students with hands-on learning experiences. Each lab is equipped with modern apparatus, chemicals, specimens, and safety equipment.

Separate Physics, Chemistry & Biology labs
Modern instruments and apparatus
Safe & supervised environment
Practical examinations facility

School Library

Our Rich Library

Our library is the heart of academic life at KPS — a quiet, inspiring space housing over 8,000 books, periodicals, encyclopedias, and reference materials covering all subjects and interest areas.

The library also maintains a reading corner with newspaper and magazine subscriptions to keep students updated on current affairs.

8,000+ books and reference materials
Separate reading area for students
Daily newspapers and magazines
Digital cataloguing system

School Transport

Safe & Reliable Transport

We operate a well-maintained fleet of school buses covering all major areas of Kushinagar district. All buses are GPS-tracked for real-time monitoring, and each bus has a trained driver and a responsible attendant to ensure student safety.

GPS-tracked fleet for all routes
Trained drivers & bus attendants
Regular vehicle maintenance checks

On-campus medical room & first aid
Trained school nurse on duty
Regular doctor visits
