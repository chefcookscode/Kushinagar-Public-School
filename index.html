Our Story

A Legacy of Learning

Kushinagar Public School stands as a beacon of quality education in the Kushinagar district of Uttar Pradesh. Affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi, we believe that education is not only the acquisition of knowledge but its reflection in daily life.

Our mission is to impart in each student a mastery of communication skills, critical thinking, and compassionate values — equipping them to succeed in an ever-evolving world.
